## No Platform-Specific Strategy

### **Id**
no-platform-strategy
### **Severity**
medium
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Each platform should have specific approach
### **Indicators**
  - Same content everywhere
  - No platform-specific goals
  - Generic approach
### **Message**
No platform-specific strategy defined.
